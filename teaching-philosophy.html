My Teaching Philosophy

Education should be student-centered, project-based, and rooted in real-world problem solving. I strive to create inclusive, engaging learning environments where every student can succeed.

Student-Centered Learning

I prioritize active participation, collaboration, and critical thinking so learners can build confidence and ownership.

Technology Integration

I blend digital tools with sound pedagogy to improve engagement, creativity, and career readiness.

Assessment & Feedback

I use formative checks and summative assessments to guide instruction and provide timely, actionable feedback.

Equity & Inclusion

I differentiate instruction and provide multiple pathways for students with diverse backgrounds and learning needs.
