Module A

Module A: AI Literacy Starter Kit

The AI Literacy Starter Kit helps students use AI as a learning partner, not a shortcut. The resources support responsible AI use, student reflection, academic integrity, privacy, verification, and classroom-ready AI routines for grades 8-12.

AI should help students think better, not think less.
Use AI to support learning, not replace thinking
Ask better questions and write stronger prompts
Check AI-generated information before trusting it
Protect personal information and respect privacy
Use AI as a study coach, brainstorming partner, and feedback tool
Recognize when AI use becomes dishonest or unhelpful
