Designed for School Leaders and Teachers

These resources are built to make responsible AI and technology instruction easier to preview, discuss, and adapt. The goal is practical classroom use: clear student language, defensible guardrails, and resources that can support digital citizenship, Computer Science Foundations, CTE pathways, and career-connected learning.

Standards and Safety Note

Standards connections are provided as cautious instructional alignments. Final use should be confirmed against local district pacing, approved course placement, and teacher directions.
