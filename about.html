About Ron Aceto

From Software Leadership to Career-Connected Technology Education

Ron Aceto is a former health IT software leader now focused on helping high school students connect computer science, business technology, cybersecurity, digital ethics, and AI literacy to practical workplace problem solving.

His background includes more than 30 years in software development, implementation, analytics, client services, and health IT leadership, followed by recent classroom experience teaching Computer Science Foundations.

CTE Direction

Teaching Philosophy

Ron believes students learn technology best when lessons are practical, structured, ethical, and connected to the real decisions people make in the workplace. His classroom approach emphasizes problem solving, student ownership, verification, privacy, communication, and persistence.
